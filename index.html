How can we go ?

  • Got to nearest town , 'Banyuwangi' of east side of Java island
  • Hire guide for climb to Ijan. I pay $80 for my private guide.
  • The entrance fee is about $15. the weekday is a little cheaper
  • It takes one hour from town to the Ijan entrance.
  • You can hire the guide after you arrive the town, Ask hotel staff.
  • Check the season. You have to go ON season, not rainy season.
  • The gate in Mt Ijan opens 1 AM.
  • In my case, It takes 1.5 hour from gate to the view point, one way
  • After top , you have to down to view point, rock, rock rock. the way is Bad footing.
